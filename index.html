About AsiaPay Vietnam

AsiaPay Vietnam is a wholly-owned subsidiary of AsiaPay Limited, a leading digital payment service provider in the Asia Pacific. We deliver reliable digital payment processing services for businesses of any size, helping Vietnamese merchants to decrease operating costs, cut fraudulent transactions, and boost revenue in both local and international markets.

Founded in 2000, AsiaPay is a premier digital payment solution and technology vendor, strive to bring advanced, secured, integrated, and cost-effective digital payment processing solutions and services to banks and eBusinesses around the world. Our services are in abundance, covering international credit and debit cards, bank account / net-banking, digital wallets, prepaid cards, over-the-counters and etc.

AsiaPay is an accredited payment processor and payment gateway solution vendor for banks, a certified IPSP for merchants and certified international 3-D Secure vendor for Visa, Mastercard, American Express, JCB, Discover Global Network and UnionPay.

AsiaPay offers a variety of award-winning, multi-currency, multi-lingual, multi-card, and multi-channeled payment solutions. Alongside with our advanced fraud detection and management solutions, we are an ideal payment provider.

Headquartered in Hong Kong, we offer professional digital payment solution consultancy and quality local service support across 15 operative offices in Asia-Pacific, including: Hong Kong, Australia (Melbourne and Sydney), China (Guangzhou, Shanghai and Shenzhen), India, Indonesia (Bali and Jakarta), Malaysia, Philippines, Singapore, Taiwan, Thailand, Vietnam and United Kingdom.

We developed the payment gateway PayDollar to provide merchants in Vietnam with a secure digital payment facility that would enable them to easily process digital payments through credit card such as Visa, Mastercard, JCB and American Express; online ATM card payment and other alternative payments including MoMo Pay and PayPal. Our bank partners including Vietcombank, Vietinbank, Techcombank, HDBank, MB Bank, TienPhong Bank, Vietnam International Bank.
